Groups Walkthrough

Section 1

Org Admin

A quick walk-through of key groups features to prepare for showcasing the app at Exponential. An organisation will be created and populated with activity which you will be added to prior to the event.

1
Accessing Org Admin

Tap the menu icon (≡) in the bottom-right corner of the app. In the menu, select Org Admin. This option is only visible to users with org-level admin permissions.

2
The Org Admin Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a real-time snapshot of the organisation.

3
Managing Groups

Scroll down past the dashboard to see all your groups listed. Tap the ⋮ icon on any group to reveal four options: View Details opens the group's stats page.

4
Group Detail View

Tapping View Details on a group opens its full profile. The Learning & Growth section tracks how many Courses, Microlearnings, and Challenges members have completed.

Section 2

New Challenge

How to create a new challenge within a group.

1
The Challenges Tab

Inside your group, tap the trophy icon to open the Challenges tab. Any currently active challenges appear under Live Challenges. To create a new one, tap + Add a new Challenge.

2
Picking a Challenge

Once you've chosen, move on to set a start date.

3
Setting a Start Date

After selecting a challenge, tap the date field to set when the challenge will go live. Challenges must be planned for the future, not the current day or in the past.

4
Choosing a Date from the Calendar

A calendar will appear. Tap the date you want the challenge to start, then tap Confirm. Use the arrows to navigate between months if needed.

5
Publishing the Challenge

After saving, your challenge will appear under Queued Challenges with its scheduled start date shown. From here you can Edit it if you need to make changes, or tap Publish to make it visible to your group immediately — even before the scheduled date.

6
Confirming Publication

A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you're sure. Tap Publish to confirm. Te challenge will immediately become visible to all members of the group.

Section 3

Pin a Course

Pinning a course lets group leaders surface a specific course directly in the group so everyone can explore it together and discuss it at their next meeting.

1
The Courses Tab

Inside your group, tap the graduation cap icon. Any courses already pinned by the leader appear here for members to explore. To add one, tap + Add a Course. You can also add Micro Learnings using the button below it.

2
Selecting and Pinning a Course

A course selection screen will appear. Browse the available courses and tap the one you want to assign. A checkmark will appear to confirm your selection. Add a description if you'd like or leave it blank and the default in-app description will be used. Then tap Pin course to add it to the group. Members will see it immediately in the Courses tab.

Section 4

Join Org & Group

Before you can participate in any group activity, you'll need to join your organisation and then join a group within it.

1
Joining Your Church

On the Join your church group screen, enter the 6-digit in the picture below (703902). This org has been pre-made and populated with group activity for showcasing.

2
Joining a Group

After joining, you'll land on your organisation's page showing its members and available groups. Under the Groups section, tap Join on yesHEis. You can only participate in one group at a time, but you're free to leave and rejoin a different group at any time.

3
The Activity Feed

Once inside your group, the Activity tab is shown by default. Here you'll see a live feed of:

  • Members joined
  • Newly scheduled challenges
  • Testimony shares
  • AOEs
  • Course / MicroLearning / Challenge completions
  • Playing Let's Plunge
